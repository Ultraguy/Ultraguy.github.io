Set the colors, and drag your mouse to place random pixels.
After you place a few pixels, click generate image to start having those pixels grow. (You can still place pixels, and change the color while it's generating)
The change of the color overtime is controlled by the colorChange variable.
Weird Mode makes everything more strand-y and sharp.
To clear the canvas, reload the page
Beware as it will lag a bit during the generating of the image, since it needs to do pixel-by-pixel operations
